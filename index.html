Who am I?

(Yes I'm a little shy)

I am an aspiring software developer.

I fell in love with programming after being introduced to Ruby 4 years ago through a one-month boot camp and have been programming ever since. I continued to take up H2 Computing at the Junior College level where I learnt Python and explored my passion for programming through hackathons and organising a computer science conference for ~100 secondary school students together with computing students from other schools.

I have the most experience with Python as it was the language used for my school's H2 Computing syllabus. I also use HTML and Javascript with Google App Engine's Python Standard Environment to develop webapps for hackathons, but I am always open to learning new languages, recently picking up VBA Macro and Octave.
